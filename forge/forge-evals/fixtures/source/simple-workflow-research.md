# Simple content workflow

## Objective

Create a very small workflow that turns a short source brief into a publishable summary.

## Stages

1. Intake
   - Read the source brief.
   - Extract the topic, audience, and key constraints.
   - Deliverable: `project-brief`.

2. Drafting
   - Use the project brief to draft the summary.
   - Deliverable: `draft-summary`.

3. Review
   - Check the draft for clarity, completeness, and tone.
   - If issues are found, request one revision round.
   - Deliverable: `review-notes`.

4. Finalization
   - Apply approved review changes.
   - Deliverable: `final-summary`.

## Checkpoints

- After Review, a reviewer must either approve the draft or request one revision.

## Constraints

- Keep the workflow simple.
- Do not merge Intake and Drafting.
- Limit revision to one round.
- The final output must preserve the original topic and audience.
